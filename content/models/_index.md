# Interactive Models & Forecasting Tools

Explore our suite of probability models and forecasting tools designed to predict wildlife harvest patterns across Minnesota. All models are built using extensive historical data from the Minnesota Department of Natural Resources.

## Available Models

### 🐻 **Bear Harvest Forecasting Model**
*Status: In Development*

Predicts bear harvest success and patterns using 26 years of historical data (2000-2026). The model incorporates seasonal trends, geographic factors, and environmental variables.

**Key Features:**
- Regional harvest predictions
- Success probability estimation
- Historical trend analysis
- Environmental factor weighting

---

### 🦌 **Deer Harvest Prediction Model**
*Status: Planned*

Comprehensive deer harvest forecasting model incorporating multiple seasons, hunting methods, and regional variations across Minnesota.

**Planned Features:**
- Archery, firearm, and muzzleloader season forecasts
- Regional population estimates
- Weather impact analysis
- Hunter success probability

---

### 🦃 **Turkey Harvest Model**
*Status: Planned*

Spring and fall turkey harvest prediction model focusing on population dynamics and harvest success rates.

**Planned Features:**
- Seasonal forecast comparison
- Regional population trends
- Breeding success indicators
- Harvest timing optimization

---

## Model Development Process

Our models follow a rigorous development and validation process:

1. **Data Collection & Cleaning** - Comprehensive historical data preparation
2. **Exploratory Analysis** - Pattern identification and feature selection
3. **Model Development** - Statistical modeling and machine learning
4. **Validation & Testing** - Cross-validation and performance assessment
5. **Deployment** - Interactive tools and real-time forecasting

## Getting Started

Models are being actively developed and will be deployed as interactive tools. Check back regularly for updates, or follow our [development blog](/posts/) for progress updates.
