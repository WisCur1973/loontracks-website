Methodology

  • Structural Model (Elastic Net): dem_share = 48.1 + 0.41 × partisan_lean + 7.1 × incumbency

  • Incumbency Bonus: +7.1 points for incumbent party (trained on 2022-2024 data)
  • Open Seats: Higher uncertainty, no incumbency advantage
  • Poll Blending: Structural 90% at 180+ days → 20% at 7 days
  • Monte Carlo: 10,000 simulations with within-state (0.85) and cross-state (0.60) correlations
  • Time-Varying Uncertainty: Error SD increases from ~4.7 near election to ~8.9 one year out
